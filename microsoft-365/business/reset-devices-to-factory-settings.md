<span data-ttu-id="3c5ea-p101">"恢复出厂设置"会将设备恢复为购买设备时的原始设置。这将删除购买设备后安装的所有应用和数据。可使用 Microsoft 365 商业版 将托管的 Windows 10 设备恢复为出厂设置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3c5ea-p101">A factory reset reverts a device to the original settings it had when the device was purchased. All apps and data on the device that were installed after purchase are removed. You can use Microsoft 365 Business to factory reset Windows 10 devices you manage.</span></span>
  
1. <span data-ttu-id="3c5ea-108">转到管理中心: <a href="https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=837890" target="_blank">https://admin.microsoft.com</a>。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3c5ea-108">Go to the admin center at <a href="https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=837890" target="_blank">https://admin.microsoft.com</a>..</span></span> 
    
2. <span data-ttu-id="3c5ea-109">在左侧导航中, 选择 "**设备** \> **管理**"。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3c5ea-109">In the left nav, choose **Devices** \> **Manage**.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="3c5ea-110">在 "**管理**" 页上, 选中要从中删除数据的设备旁边的复选框, 然后在 "**管理**" 下拉框中选择 "**恢复**"。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3c5ea-110">On the **Manage** page, check the checkbox next to the device you want to remove data from and then, in the **Manage** drop-down choose **Factory reset**.</span></span>
    
4. <span data-ttu-id="3c5ea-111">在" **确定将以下设备恢复为出厂设置**"窗格中，选择" **确认**"\>" **关闭**"。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3c5ea-111">On the **Are you sure you want to factory reset the devices below** pane, choose **Confirm** \> **Close**.</span></span>
