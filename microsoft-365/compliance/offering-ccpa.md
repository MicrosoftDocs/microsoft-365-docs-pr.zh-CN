# <a name="california-consumer-privacy-act-ccpa"></a><span data-ttu-id="22a8c-104">加州消费者隐私法案 (CCPA)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-104">California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)</span></span>

## <a name="ccpa-overview"></a><span data-ttu-id="22a8c-105">CCPA 概述</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-105">CCPA overview</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-106">加利福尼亚州的消费者隐私法案 (CCPA) 是美国的第一项全面隐私法律。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-106">The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) is the first comprehensive privacy law in the United States.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-107">它为加利福尼亚消费者提供了各种隐私权限。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-107">It provides a variety of privacy rights to California consumers.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-108">由 CCPA 管控的企业将对这些使用者有很多义务，包括披露的常规数据保护条例、) 的使用者数据 (保护条例 (Dsr) 、"自愿退出" 以进行某些数据传输以及对未成年人的 "选择性加入" 的要求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-108">Businesses regulated by the CCPA will have a number of obligations to those consumers, including disclosures, General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)-like consumer data subject rights (DSRs), an 'opt-out' for certain data transfers, and an 'opt-in' requirement for minors.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-109">CCPA 仅适用于在加利福尼亚开展生意的公司，满足以下条件中的一个或多个： (1) 的年收入超过 $25000000，或 (2) 从销售加利福尼亚消费者个人信息中获取年收入超过50%，或者 (3) 购买、销售或共享超过50000个加州消费者的个人信息。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-109">The CCPA only applies to companies doing business in California which satisfy one or more of the following: (1) have a gross annual revenue of more than $25 million, or (2) derive more than 50% of their annual income from the sale of California consumer personal information, or (3) buy, sell or share the personal information of more than 50,000 California consumers annually.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-110">CCPA 将于 2020 年 1 月 1 日生效。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-110">The CCPA goes into effect on January 1, 2020.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-111">但是，由加利福尼亚律师常规 (AG) 强制实施将于2020年7月1日开始。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-111">However, enforcement by the California Attorney General (AG) will start on July 1, 2020.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-112">加利福尼亚州的 AG 将强制实施 CCPA，并且将有能力发出不合规性罚款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-112">The California AG will enforce the CCPA and will have power to issue non-compliance fines.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-113">CCPA 还提供了仅限于数据泄露的私有操作权限。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-113">The CCPA also provides a private right of action which is limited to data breaches.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-114">私人诉权规定，每位消费者在每个事件中受到的损失可能介于 100 美元到 750 美元之间。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-114">Under the private right of action, damages can come in between $100 and $750 per incident per consumer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-115">加州司法部长还可在整体上实施 CCPA，可就每次违规征收不超过 2,500 美元的民事罚款，就每次故意违规征收 7,500 的的民事罚款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-115">The California AG also can enforce the CCPA in its entirety with the ability to levy a civil penalty of not more than $2,500 per violation or $7,500 per intentional violation.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-117">对于在加利福尼亚开展业务的商业客户，Microsoft 将作为 "服务提供商"，与我们的在线服务和专业服务产品有关。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-117">For commercial customers doing business in California, Microsoft will be acting as a 'service provider' with respect to our Online Services and Professional Services offering.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-118">在线服务条款 (OST) 和 Microsoft 专业服务数据保护附录 (MSDPA) 已经满足 CCPA 下的服务提供商的要求，并且通常足以允许客户继续将数据传输到我们的在线服务。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-118">The terms of the Online Services Terms (OST) and the Microsoft Professional Services Data Protection Addendum (MSDPA) already meet the requirements for Service Providers under the CCPA and are generally sufficient to permit customers to continue to transfer data to our Online Services.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-119">因此，客户无需进行其他合约更改即可将 Microsoft 作为 CCPA 下的服务提供商。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-119">As such, no additional contractual changes are required for customers to be able to rely on Microsoft as a Service Provider under the CCPA.</span></span>

## <a name="frequently-asked-questions"></a><span data-ttu-id="22a8c-135">常见问题解答</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-135">Frequently asked questions</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-136">**CCPA 将如何影响我的公司？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-136">**How will the CCPA affect my company?**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-137">CCPA 对 Californians 的许多权限都与 GDPR 提供的权限相似，包括泄露和数据主体权限 (DSR) 请求，例如访问、删除和可移植性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-137">Many of the CCPA’s rights afforded to Californians are similar to the rights the GDPR provides, including the disclosure and data subject right (DSR) requests, such as access, deletion, and portability.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-138">因此，客户可以查看我们现有的 GDPR 解决方案，以帮助他们解决其 CCPA 合规性问题。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-138">As such, customer can look to our already existing GDPR solutions to help them with their CCPA compliance.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-139">若要开始您的 CCPA 旅程，您应重点关注信息的发现，确定个人信息的共享方式、控制其使用方式、保护方式以及将正式的数据泄露响应程序设置为正确的方式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-139">To begin your CCPA journey you should focus on Discovery of information, determining how personal information is shared, governing how it is used, how it is protected and having a formal data breach response program in place.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-140">**GDPR 与 CCPA 之间有何区别？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-140">**What are the differences between GDPR and CCPA?**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-141">两者存在诸多差异。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-141">There are many differences.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-142">更容易着重关注相似之处，包括：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-142">It’s easier to focus on the similarities, including:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-143">透明度/泄露义务</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-143">Transparency/disclosure obligations,</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-144">访问、删除和接收数据副本的使用者权限</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-144">Consumer rights to access, delete, and receive a copy of data,</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-147">CCPA 中的最大区别是启用从数据销售到第三方 (的核心要求是，通过广泛定义的 "销售" 来包括数据共享，以便在很有价值的考虑) 。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-147">The biggest difference in CCPA is the core requirement to enable an opt-out from sales of data to third parties (with 'sale' broadly defined to include sharing of data for valuable consideration).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-148">**根据 CCPA 的规定，公司必须落实哪些权利？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-148">**What rights must companies enable under the CCPA?**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-149">CCPA 需要管控企业来收集、转移和销售个人信息，以及其他一些内容：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-149">The CCPA requires regulated businesses that collect, transfer, and sell personal information to, among other things:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-151">提供与收集的个人信息的来源、业务用途和类别相关的隐私策略中更详细的披露，包括如何将这些类别出售或转移到其他实体。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-151">Provide more detailed disclosures in a privacy policy regarding the sources, business purposes, and categories of personal information that is collected, including how those categories are sold or transferred to other entities.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="22a8c-157">**CCPA 如何适用于儿童？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-157">**How does the CCPA apply to children?**</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="22a8c-158">针对未满 13 岁的儿童，CCPA 引入了与美国《儿童在线隐私保护法案》(COPPA) 相符的家长同意义务。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="22a8c-158">CCPA introduces parental consent obligations consistent with The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) for children under the age of 13.</span></span>
