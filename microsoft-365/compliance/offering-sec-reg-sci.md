<span data-ttu-id="0ded7-106">美国证券和外汇委员会（SEC）是美国联邦政府的独立机构以及美国证券市场的主要 overseer 和调压器。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0ded7-106">The US Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) is an independent agency of the US federal government and the primary overseer and regulator of US securities markets.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0ded7-107">它 wields 强制实施政府证券法，提出新的证券规则，并监督证券业的市场法规。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0ded7-107">It wields enforcement authority over federal securities laws, proposes new securities rules, and oversees market regulation of the securities industry.</span></span>
