## <a name="the-principles-of-retention-or-what-takes-precedence"></a><span data-ttu-id="3ff4a-343">保留原则或优先级</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3ff4a-343">The principles of retention, or what takes precedence?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3ff4a-344">与保留标签不同，您可以对同一内容应用多个保留策略。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3ff4a-344">Unlike retention labels, you can apply more than one retention policy to the same content.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3ff4a-345">每个保留策略都可以导致保留操作和删除操作。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3ff4a-345">Each retention policy can result in a retain action and a delete action.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3ff4a-346">此外，该商品还可能受到保留标签上的这些措施的约束。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3ff4a-346">Additionally, that item could also be subject to these actions from a retention label.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3ff4a-347">在这种情况下，当项目可以经受可能彼此冲突的多个保留设置时，确定结果的优先顺序是什么？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3ff4a-347">In this scenario, when items can be subject to multiple retention settings that could conflict with one another, what takes precedence to determine the outcome?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3ff4a-350">例如，一个项目可能要受一个已配置为仅删除操作的保留策略的约束，而另一个则要配置为保留然后删除的保留策略。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3ff4a-350">For example, an item might be subject to one retention policy that is configured for a delete-only action, and another retention policy that is configured to retain and then delete.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3ff4a-351">因此，此项只有一个保留操作和两个删除操作。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3ff4a-351">Consequently, this item has just one retain action but two delete actions.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3ff4a-352">保留和删除操作可能彼此冲突，并且这两个删除操作的日期可能冲突。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3ff4a-352">The retention and deletion actions could be in conflict with one another and the two deletion actions might have a conflicting date.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3ff4a-353">为了得出结果，你必须运用保留原则。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3ff4a-353">To work out the outcome, you must apply the principles of retention.</span></span>
